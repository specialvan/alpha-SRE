# New Requirement Execution Flow

1. Propose the requirement.
2. Classify the requirement into a layer.
3. Confirm the operational boundary.
4. Decide whether the requirement is baseline, increment, experimental, or archive.
5. Implement the smallest safe change.
6. Test the change.
7. Update the relevant documents.
8. Verify rollback and review readiness.
9. Archive or continue the item.
